A Husband and Wife's Dream Café

As the owners of Das Green Café, James Green and Antara Das, we crafted a culinary haven that caters to individuals with diverse religious and health dietary restrictions. Being a multicultured couple, we always had a hard time finding food that fit both of our personal dietary restrictions. My wife, Antara, is a Bengali Hindu that cannot eat beef, nor eat any food that has beef broth or any other beef product. And I, James, can't eat anything with dairy and hates any kind of cheese. Any time I tried to get anything cheeseless, I would always be offered vegan cheese. I just don't like cheese!

Our dream is to create a Café that hosts many alternatives that can provide a good dish for anyone regardless of what foods they can or can't eat. With a deep understanding and respect for the importance of accommodating different dietary needs, we have meticulously curated a menu that offers an array of options to suit various preferences. Whether it be vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, or kosher, every customer can find a delightful selection of dishes that align with their dietary requirements.

Our commitment to sourcing fresh, locally grown produce and utilizing sustainable ingredients not only promotes healthy eating but also aligns with their dedication to the environment.
